Who am I?

I have always been a curious and detail-oriented person, craving to understand the “how” and “why” behind everything I encountered. The combination of my desire to create and my childhood hobbies led me to a BFA in Fashion Design from Otis College of Art and Design. My years as an apparel designer for Hurley, Abercombie and Fitch, and Tea Collection allowed me to see a product from concept to production and into the hands of customers, but I wanted something more. While I love the skills I developed as an apparel designer, I learned that I have a greater curiosity for function, customer interaction, and the ever-changing world of design. I decided to take the leap and pursue a career in UI/UX to learn, understand, and design in an industry that is filled with excitement and endless opportunities.

When I’m not designing, I can be found playing with my corgi Kuma, fiddling on the piano, drawing on my iPad, or wandering the streets of an unfamiliar city with a camera and passport in hand. I take exploring very seriously, always striving to learn and discover something new even if I take the same path every day.
