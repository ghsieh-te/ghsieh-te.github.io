Who am I?

Growing up, I was always known as the child who dived head first into every art and craft project with curiosity and passion. I found all the opportunities to fill the blank space in the margins of my homework with doodles and spent more time drawing a cover for my book report than writing the actual report. While my respect for the blank margins on my homework grew with the encouragement of my teachers after a few years in school, my curiosity and desire to create never vanished. That desire to design led me to complete a BFA in Fashion Design at Otis College of Art and Design years later and has shaped my career over time.

I worked for a few years in the fashion industry as an apparel designer for Abercrombie and Fitch and then for Tea Collection when I moved back to the SF Bay Area. While I love the skills I learned as an apparel designer, I learned I have a greater curiosity for function and the ever-changing world of digital design. I decided to take the leap and pursue a career in UI/UX to learn, understand, and design in an industry that is filled with excitement and endless opportunities.
